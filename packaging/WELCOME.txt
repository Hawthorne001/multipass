Multipass: Ubuntu at your fingertips.

Welcome to the Multipass installer. This will set up Multipass on your computer.

Multipass gives you Ubuntu VMs in seconds. Just run `multipass launch` and it'll do all the setup for you.

For more information, visit our website https://github.com/CanonicalLtd/multipass


You will be guided through the steps necessary to install this software.
